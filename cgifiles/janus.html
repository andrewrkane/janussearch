The Janus Intertextuality Search Engine

a research tool for

The Electronic Manipulus florum Project & The Digital Liber pharetrae Project & The Digital Viridarium consolationis Project

For single word searches, including the cited author or source, use the keyword search field. For searches of texts having a minimum of 20 characters, enter or paste it into the Intertextuality Search field. For Intertextuality Searches, two types of report are available: the "Excerpt Report" displays only the corresponding passage from your text with the matching quotations from the Manipulus florum and/or the Liber pharetrae with the common text highlighted; the "Document Report" displays your entire search document with the common text highlighted. The default setting is for both reports, so users may choose to limit the report for their search to one or the other type. For an Intertextuality Report showing the quotations from one source that appear in the other sources see Manipulus florum, Liber pharetrae, Viridarium consolationis.
